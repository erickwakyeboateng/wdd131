Top Tourist Attractions

Explore the breathtaking landscapes, exotic wildlife, and monumental historical sites that make Ghana the crown jewel of West African tourism.

History

Cape Coast Castle

A UNESCO World Heritage Site with a profound history, standing as a poignant reminder of the transatlantic slave trade. A must-visit for historical education and reflection.

Nature

Kakum National Park

Experience the thrill of walking on one of the few canopy walkways in the world, suspended 30 meters above the lush rainforest floor, surrounded by rare flora and fauna.

Wildlife

Mole National Park

Embark on an unforgettable safari in Ghana's largest wildlife refuge. Spot magnificent African elephants, antelopes, baboons, and hundreds of bird species in their natural habitat.
