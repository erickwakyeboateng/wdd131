Discover Ghana - Website Plan

Site Name

Discover Ghana

This name represents a website focused on showcasing Ghana’s culture, tourism, and heritage in an engaging and informative way.

Site Purpose

The website provides an overview of Ghana’s culture, traditions, and major tourist attractions. It serves as an informational platform for users who want to learn about Ghana and explore its key highlights.

Scenarios

  • What are the top tourist attractions to visit in Ghana?
  • What cultural traditions and festivals are practiced in Ghana?

Color Schema

  • Green (#006B3F) – Headings, header, footer
  • Gold (#FCD116) – Accents and borders
  • Red (#CE1126) – Highlights
  • Light Background (#F9F9F9) – Page background

Typography

  • Roboto Slab – Headings
  • Roboto – Body text

Wireframes

Content Structure

  • Home Page – Introduction to Ghana
  • Tourist Attractions – Cape Coast Castle, Kakum National Park, Mole National Park
  • Culture – Traditions, festivals, clothing, and food
  • Gallery – Image showcase
  • Contact Page – User feedback form
